**Verdict** is an essay written by [[Jasnah Kholin\|Jasnah Kholin]] in the year 1174. It is one of three arguments presented to the [[/wiki/Azir#Government]] by the [[Coalition of monarchs\|coalition of monarchs]] in an attempt to get the central  empire to join their forces.
*Verdict* is the shortest essay of the three, being only a single, double-sided page long. It is written entirely in single rhythmic meter and includes references to [[Aqqu\|Aqqu's seven Logical Forms]] and the Grand Orientation. It also contains a quote by [[Kasimarlix\|Prime Kasimarlix]] that is used to extrapolate to different levels of Superior Understanding, in three sequential stages.
The essay was presented to the Azish  and  by [[Dalinar Kholin\|Dalinar Kholin]], in an effort to convince the country to join the coalition against [[Odium\|Odium]] and the [[Fused\|Fused]]. It was the final essay to be presented and convinced Vizier [[Noura\|Noura]] to at least consider the alliance offer and present the arguments to the [[Prime Aqasix\|Prime]]. *Verdict* was received favorably by the officials and they were highly impressed by Jasnah's writing, finding it to live up to her reputation as a scholar. Nevertheless, it was not Jasnah's essay, but [[Navani\|Navani]]'s, that eventually convinced the Azish to join the coalition.

## Trivia
'Verdict' is the chapter title in which the three essays are presented to the Azish.
