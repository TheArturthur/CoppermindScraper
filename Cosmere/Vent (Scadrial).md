**Vent** is a [[Skaa\|skaa]] who lived in the [[Final Empire\|Final Empire]] on [[Scadrial\|Scadrial]]. He is an extremely nervous and paranoid man.
Vent knew [[Kelsier\|Kelsier]] and [[Dockson\|Dockson]] and may have worked with them at some point. When Kelsier met Vin, she was very paranoid from her life in the thieving crews, reminding Kelsier of Vent. Dockson insisted that Vin was not as bad, and he and Kelsier chuckled about Vent's jumpiness. As Kelsier and Dockson spoke of Vent in the past tense, it seems likely that he was dead or no longer in contact with them by the time they met Vin.
