**ShalRim** is a  fabric on [[Taldain\|Taldain]]. It is known for being strong as well as comfortable. [[Raagent\|Raagent]] preferred ShalRim over  materials for his bed sheets. The material is strong enough that [[Sharezan\|Sharezan]] was able to use Raagent's ShalRim blanket to securely tie him up.
