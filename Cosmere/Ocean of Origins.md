The **Ocean of Origins** is a body of water east of the main continent on [[Roshar\|Roshar]], towards the [[Origin\|Origin of Storms]].

## Geography
The shores of the Ocean of Origins are sparsely populated, with the cities of [[New Natanan\|New Natanan]] and [[Dawn's Shadow\|Dawn's Shadow]] being the only notable settlements. This is likely because [[Highstorm\|highstorms]] make landfall there, and as such hit that coast with the greatest force. To the east, the Ocean of Origins presumably connects to the [[Endless Ocean\|Endless Ocean]] on the opposite side of the planet, while from the north and south, it's bracketed by the [[Steamwater Ocean\|Steamwater Ocean]] and the [[Southern Depths\|Southern Depths]].

## Culture
The Origin, where the highstorms originate, is said to lie at the far end of the Ocean of Origins. Both [[Singer\|singers]] and humans strongly associate the Origin with the east, including the ocean. Some older legends claimed that the [[Voidbringer\|Voidbringers]] originated somewhere in the Ocean of Origins. Modern legends claim that there is an inhabited island at the Origin, populated by people called the [[Uvara\|Uvara]] in some versions of the story. The version told in New Natanan says that they are sailors who were lost while traveling the Ocean of Origins and that one day, they will come back to reclaim [[Natanatan\|Natanatan]].

## History
[[Dalinar\|Dalinar]] sailed across the Ocean of Origins when taking his forces to the conflict that would become the [[War of Reckoning\|War of Reckoning]]. Several years later, [[Shallan\|Shallan]] and [[Jasnah\|Jasnah]] planned to sail through it all the way to [[New Natanan\|New Natanan]], but their ship was destroyed before reaching it. After the arrival of the [[Everstorm\|Everstorm]], it and the [[Highstorm\|highstorms]] often clashed over either the Ocean of Origins or the [[Endless Ocean\|Endless Ocean]].
