**Owen** is one of [[Stephen Leeds\|Stephen Leeds]]' .
They are a fan of golf, and convince another of Stephen's aspects [[Clive\|Clive]] to take up the sport. After the  meeting, [[Tobias\|Tobias]] concludes that Owen's theory is one of the more plausible.
They have a theory about ghosts which Stephen thinks is outlandish, potentially a theory about what the aspects are.
