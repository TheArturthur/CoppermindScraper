**Drenya** is a woman kidnapped by the [[Set\|Set]] on [[Scadrial\|Scadrial]]. She would end up living in the [[Community\|Community]] project. When [[Marasi\|Marasi]] arrives, Drenya is one of the people to gather at [[Armal\|Armal]]’s house to greet her. She was likely freed with the others after the draining of the [[Perpendicularity\|perpendicularity]].
