## Appearance and Personality
She is a twenty-something year old woman.
Sefwaynn has short, blond hair in tight curls, that don't quite reach her shoulders. She wears a handmade, long-sleeved white dress, with a shorter, black, sleeveless dress over the top. It has some maroon embroidery around the neck, with intentionally frayed white ropes tied around her waist. She has a steely-eyed glare.
Sefawynn bears the [[Night Marks\|Night Marks]], a birthmark of three dark blue spots which is seen as a sign of being chosen or cursed by Woden.
Sefawynn doubts her own abilities as a skop; while she can see [[Wight\|wights]] invisible to other people, her boasts do not seem to take effect, so she considers herself essentially a grifter.

## Attributes and Abilities
Sefawynn is skilled at tying knots, and is a [[Skop\|skop]].

## History
### Pre Outlander Arrival
Sefawynn and [[Wyrm\|Wyrm]] had already left [[Stenford\|Stenford]] by the time [[John West\|John West]] arrived in their dimension. After inspecting the human-shaped burn mark in a field outside the village, [[Ealstan\|Ealstan]] ordered [[Hild\|Hild]] to send a boy to chase them down, and get them to return to Stenford, as they would require her services as a [[Skop\|skop]] again. Hild suggested that Sefawynn would like that.

### Outlanders in Stenford
Sefawynn and Wyrm crept up behind John West, keeping to the shadows, as he was outside eavesdropping on [[Ulric Stromfin\|Ulric's]] meeting with Ealstan in the meeting hall. Sefawynn appeared panicked, and pointed at John. While Wyrm attacked John with a knife, despite his slashes not cutting John, Sefawynn found a wooden a board and swung it at John's face, knocking him to the ground.

>“*I banished the beast of Bastion's Barrow. Song-sounder I am and sing out strongest.*”
\-Sefawynn attempts to bind John with a boast[4]

Believing him to be an [[Aelv\|aelv]], Sefawynn and Wyrm bound John's legs tightly and hung him, unconscious and upside down, from a ceiling beam, with his hands tied behind his back. Sefawynn waited, watching him, until he finally opened his eyes. She didn't respond as John taunted her. Wyrm mentioned to Sefawynn that he didn't think hanging John upside down seemed to be working. Sefawynn disagreed, as John hadn't eaten them yet. John demanded to be released, under the threat of burning down their house. Unfazed, Sefawynn raised up both of her hands, with the fingers up and thumbs out toward one another. She spoke her first boast. Neither she nor Wyrm could see that it had any effect on John, so Sefawynn, making the same hand gesture, tried a second, stronger boast. John didn't even flinch. Slightly unsure of herself, she asked Wyrm to fetch Ealstan, and reassured Wyrm that she would be fine on her own. While Wyrm was gone, Sefawynn ignored all of John's questions, glaring at him and keeping her face expressionless. She watched on as John struggled to untie himself.
Eventually, Wyrm returned with Ealstan in tow. Sefawynn admitted to Ealstan that she was unsure how to deal with John. Noting the fact that they could see him, they ruled out that he was a [[Landswight\|landswight]], but Ealstan took Sefawynn at her word that John was an aelv. After John taunted Ealstan, asking for an impossible list of items to apologize for his treatment, Ealstan asked Sefawynn if she wanted to stay with John, or come with him to speak with Ulric and Quinn. Sefawynn said she would stay with John, but asked Ealstan to take Wyrm with him, and to send her brother back if she was needed. As soon as Ealstan and Wyrm had left, Sefawynn confronted John, and told him to cut the lies because she knew what he really was.

## Relationships
### John West
Sefawynn met [[John West\|John West]] when she and her brother captured John shortly after his arrival. She decided he must be a charlatan rather than an aelv, despite his appearance. She comes to respect him as they travel together, attempting to rescue her brother [[Wyrm\|Wyrm]].
As they continued to travel together, Sefawynn and John fell in love. When the [[Hordamen\|Hordamen]] launched a major invasion of [[Maelport\|Maelport]], she defied [[Woden\|Woden]] and her boasts inspired the wights to attack the Hordamen. After the battle, [[Logna\|Logna]] told John that his other-dimensional nature would protect Sefawynn from Woden's retaliation, and they eventually married.
