**Mikels** is a soldier who is in [[Hershel\|Hershel]]'s squad during the raid on a Russian satellite, when [[Calamity\|Calamity]] granted powers to all the . He likely acts as the squad’s demolitions expert. When the raid goes wrong and they are discovered, Hershel orders Mikels to blow up the facility. However, [[Steelheart\|Steelheart]] soon attacks the squad, and Mikels was subsequently killed.
