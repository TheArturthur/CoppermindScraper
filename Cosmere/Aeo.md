**Aeo** is a [[Seon\|seon]] on [[Sel\|Sel]]. Aeo and [[Ati (seon)\|Ati's]] masters both died at some point between the [[Reod\|Reod]] and the restoration of [[Elantris (city)\|Elantris]]. After the two seons recovered, [[Raoden\|Raoden]] decided to give them to [[Dashe\|Dashe]], who in turn gave Aeo to his daughter [[Matisse\|Matisse]]. The seon's name comes from [[/wiki/Aon#Aeo]], which means bravery; [[Ashe\|Ashe]] and Raoden agree that Aeo is a good fit for Matisse.
