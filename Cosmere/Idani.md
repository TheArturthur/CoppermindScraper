**Idani** is an [[Alethi\|Alethi]] woman on [[Roshar\|Roshar]]. At the age of fourteen, Idani is beautiful and more mature than is typical. She is slightly older than [[Adolin Kholin\|Adolin Kholin]] and is a cousin to [[Halam\|Halam]], [[Aratin\|Aratin]], and [[Meleran Khal\|Meleran Khal]].

## History
Idani had just moved to [[Kholinar\|Kholinar]] sometime around the year 1164. Adolin had a crush on her, so when she talked to him about wanting to see him use his sword, he bought her a [[Ulius\|sword]] as a gift, completely missing the innuendo in her comments. Adolin tried to give the sword to Idani, but she grew bored with his hour-long explanation of its virtues and left without taking it. It was later on that, while Idani was attempting to bond with others by making fun of [[Renarin\|Renarin]], Adolin overheard her and learned a larger lesson about people being able to be both beautiful and ugly.
Adolin recounted the details of his interactions with Idani to [[Shallan Davar\|Shallan Davar]] in the year 1175 when telling her about his first crush. Looking back, he could understand why Idani acted like she did and used the insight to lead into a conversation about his relationship with his [[Dalinar Kholin\|father]].
