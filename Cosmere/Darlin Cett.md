**Darlin Cett** is a member of [[House Cett\|House Cett]] on [[Scadrial\|Scadrial]]. He is either the Lord of House Cett during 348 [[Catacendre\|PC]], or acts as the representative of House Cett on the Elendel Senate.

## Appearence and Personality
Darlin Cett is a well dressed man, with slicked back, thinning hair. He also uses powders, likely to whiten his skin as is fashionable during 348 [[Catacendre\|PC]]. Darlin is knowledgable about Elendel politics, and he is somewhat of a war hawk, believing that they should deal the first strike to the Outer Cities. However, after realising that there was a bomb pointed at [[Elendel\|Elendel]], he immediately made arrangements to flee, valuing his own life above those of the common people.

## History
Darlin was the senator for House Cett during 348 [[Catacendre\|PC]]. After [[Steris Ladrian\|Steris Ladrian]] asked the Senate to approve of a [[/wiki/Elendel#Senate]] to deal with the issue of the Outer Cities, Darlin voted in favour of this proposal, knowing that he would be included in the council. During the meeting this council held, Darlin argued for a preemptive strike against the Outer Cities, believing that if they waited, they soon wouldn’t be able to win anymore. After receiving a message detailing the presence of an Outer Cities’ bomb that was pointed at Elendel, Darlin immediately made arrangements to flee the city.
