**Jabom** is a town in southern [[Marat\|Marat]] on [[Roshar\|Roshar]], located along the coast of the [[Southern Depths\|Southern Depths]]. It is east of [[Rossen Dar\|Rossen Dar]], but west of [[Ja Dran\|Ja Dran]].
