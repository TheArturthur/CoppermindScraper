The **Islands of Lobu** are a group of islands, located within the [[Sapphire Sea\|Sapphire Sea]] on [[Lumar\|Lumar]]. It is where [[Fort\|Fort]] and [[Salay\|Salay]] are from.
There is a culture of people who are called the **hunters** who come from the Islands of Lobu. Their tenet is that they must search for a worthy hunt. They hunt likely used to be actual animal or treasure hunting, however their definition broadened with the years. When a person comes of age they choose their hunt. It can be anything, like baking bread, and planning weddings. Fort's and his mother's hunt was trading. If someone is off the islands, they send letters to people talking about their hunt, to be recorded and hung on the wall.
