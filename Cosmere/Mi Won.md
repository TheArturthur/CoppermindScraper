**Mi Won** is one of [[Stephen Leeds\|Stephen Leeds]]' .
She is Korean, and is Stephen's surgeon and field medic. During the  she analyzes the effects of inserting extra DNA into muscle cells.
Along with [[Clive\|Clive]] and [[Arnaud\|Arnaud]], Mi Won is among the aspects best equipped to think about higher-level chemistry. She concludes that  and [[Yol Chay\|Yol Chay]] are keeping information from Stephen.
Later, when Stephen began to lose control of his aspects, Mi Won was the first to arrive when [[Armando\|Armando]] died. She left the room and told [[Ivy\|Ivy]] of the death. Ivy then pushed into the room and demanded more details.
