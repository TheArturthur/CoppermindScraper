**Norweg** was a kingdom in the [[Earth (Frugal Wizard)\|alternate medieval dimension]]. It was in the same region as [[Hordaland\|Hordaland]], [[Dansic\|Dansic]], and the [[Geats\|Geats]], and was located across the ocean from [[Weswara\|Weswara]]. It was formerly independent, but was conquered and enslaved by the [[Hordamen\|Hordamen]]. It is likely that Norweg is the medieval dimension's version of Norway.
When [[John West\|John West]] was asking [[Sefawynn\|Sefawynn]] and [[Ealstan\|Ealstan]] where the [[Hordamen\|Hordamen]] came from, he suggested countries such as Denmark, Norway and Sweden as possibilities. Ealstan responded that they had, in fact, come from [[Hordaland\|Hordaland]], and he didn't seem to recognize the countries that John mentioned. Sefawynn, however, seemed to notice the similarities in country names between those that John mentioned, and Norweg and Dansic in her dimension.
