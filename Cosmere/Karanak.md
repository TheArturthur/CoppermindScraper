**Karanak** is a major city in southern [[Alethkar\|Alethkar]] in the [[Bethab princedom\|Bethab princedom]]. During the [[True Desolation\|True Desolation]], Odium’s forces advanced on Karanak and captured it after a long and bloody siege.

## Geography
Karanak is located in the [[Bethab princedom\|Bethab princedom]]. It sits near [[Mevan Bay\|Mevan Bay]] and the delta where the [[Deathbend River\|Deathbend River]] meets the [[Tarat Sea\|Tarat Sea]]. These floodplains are rocky and strewn with rubble. The city is known to have an old seaside passage. The city has multiple Vorin temples, one of which is dedicated to [[Jezrien\|Jezrien]]. This temple has a serene courtyard, with a small fountain. As a major port, Karanak is of great strategic and economic importance.

## History
### True Desolation
In the year following the [[Battle of Thaylen Field\|Battle of Thaylen Field]], [[Coalition of monarchs\|coalition]] forces took control of southwestern Alethkar along the coast of the Tarat Sea. Karanak was under coalition control at the time of [[Taravangian\|Taravangian's]] betrayal and coalition forces in southern Alethkar successfully regrouped at the city with minimal casualties. Not long after however, the [[Fused\|Fused]] closed in on the city, laying siege to it. The defenders were commanded by General [[Jathid\|Jathid]].
The siege lasted many months, with nearly the entire delta around Karanak becoming a battlefield. During the siege, the defenders often clashed with a violent singer who called himself “Brightlord” [[Hathalash\|Hathalash]]. At some point during the siege, a violent [[Stormform\|stormform]] [[Regal\|Regal]] tried to assassinate the city’s only [[Soulcaster\|Soulcaster]], . The attempt failed, but it shook the men as the attack showcased how close the city was to falling. A week after the assassination attempt, a singer named  escaped from Odium’s camp and fled to the city. Jathid heard of this, and sent a group to rescue her and bring her safely back to the city gates. Despite encountering a stormform Regal, they were successful.
Jathid also received news that the enemy would commit their troops during a midday assault against the defenders. He spoke to Kethrin and let the group that rescued her choose whether they would attempt to defend the city’s Soulcaster, or rescue Kethrin’s still imprisoned kin. It is unclear what they chose. Regardless, Jathid committed a contingent of his personal guard to complete the other mission. In the morning, a [[Highstorm\|highstorm]] passed over the city, and not long after Jathid sent his troops to confront the enemy. As Odium’s forces launched their assault, and were met with heavy resistance, Tira hid in a [[Vorin\|Vorin]] temple. This temple, dedicated to the [[Herald\|Herald]] [[Jezrien\|Jezrien]], was infiltrated by three singers. Two warform soldiers cut off her escape routes while a soldier in stormform attacked her. The singers and Tira’s guards fought, but it is unclear who won.
Meanwhile, a group of coalition soldiers attempted to rescue Kethrin’s kin. They snuck around the battle and entered the enemy camp. There, they were ambushed by two warforms and a [[Nightform\|nightform]] singer. They are known to have made it back to the city with the imprisoned singers. On the field of battle, the singers committed a large force of warform soldiers, with archers on the flanks and stormforms as shock troops in front. The battle went well for the coalition, and Jathid returned to the city victorious. He ordered that the rescued singers be imprisoned in the city’s dungeons. A group of soldiers tried to convince him that they should be let free instead, but it is unclear what decision he made.
