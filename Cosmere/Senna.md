**Senna** is someone or something that [[Leras\|Leras]], the Vessel of [[Preservation\|Preservation]], mentions to [[Kelsier\|Kelsier]] in the [[Cognitive Realm\|Cognitive Realm]] after [[Ruin\|Ruin's]] release. Kelsier attempts to comfort a weakened and dying Leras, and Leras brings up Senna in a seemingly rueful manner.
Senna is not someone that Leras thinks of as a deity. If Senna is a person, they are not a love interest of [[Hoid\|Hoid's]].
