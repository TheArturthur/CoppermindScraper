## Appearance and Personality
Traiben is a friendly man with short black hair and light Daysider skin. He wears the traditional robes of a sand master. Although he is well aware of his friend [[Kenton\|Kenton's]] lack of power, he does not look down on him for it even though other mastrells consider his association with Kenton a failing.

## Attributes and Abilities
He is a powerful sand master, and is able to control fifteen ribbons of sand at the same time. He is generally well-prepared for dangerous situations.

## History
Traiben became friends with Kenton when they were both young; he was familiar with Kenton's old sand fox, [[Scooch\|Scooch]]. Kenton noted that Traiben was his oldest friend other than [[Aarik\|Aarik]]. Traiben entered the [[Diem\|Diem]] at the same time as Kenton and showed promise in sand mastery.
He remained Kenton's best friend even though he was promoted to the rank of mastrell while Kenton remained an acolent. He engaged in friendly competitions with Kenton, and he did not like [[Drile\|Drile]] and his cronies. Traiben generally supported Kenton, although he was skeptical of Kenton's decision to undergo the [[Mastrell's Path\|Mastrell's Path]]. Kenton would regularly discuss the concerns he had about the Diem and its leadership with Traiben.
After Kenton succeeded in his run and completed the Path, retrieving an unheard-of six spheres, Traiben remarked that when he ran the Path he only found three spheres; he wondered if Kenton could have found a sphere that he lost. Although [[Elorin\|Elorin]] tried convincing Kenton not to go to the advancement ceremony, Traiben encouraged Kenton to attend, reasoning that [[Praxton\|Praxton]] would have to make him a mastrell after the results of the Path. Before the advancement ceremony, Traiben and nearly all of the other sand masters drank from the ceremonial bowl that Elorin had poisoned with [[KaDo\|KaDo]]. During the ceremony, a large band of [[Kerztian\|Kerztian]] warrior-priests launched a surprise attack. Due to the KaDo, Traiben and most of the Diem were killed while defending themselves from the Kerztians. The KaDo caused Traiben to [[/wiki/Sand mastery#Overmastery]] within minutes, when he should have been able to use his powers for much longer. Kenton tried to give him some water to save him, but it was too late, and Traiben died in his arms. Kenton was extremely upset by Traiben's death and carried his memory with him, hoping that his actions to save the Diem would honor his friend.
