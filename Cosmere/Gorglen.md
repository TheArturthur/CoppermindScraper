**Gorglen** is a member of the [[Elendel\|Elendel]] constabulary on [[Scadrial\|Scadrial]]. He was a previous partner of [[Marasi\|Marasi]] before Wayne. By 348 [[Catacendre\|PC]] he was being impersonated by the [[Kandra\|kandra]] [[TenSoon\|TenSoon]], indicating he had likely died some time prior. [[Wayne\|Wayne]] believed that he was a giraffe.
