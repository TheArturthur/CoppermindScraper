**Idkwyl Elariel** is a member of [[House Elariel\|House Elariel]] in the Fourth century after the [[Catacendre\|Catacendre]]. She is an up-and-coming fashion designer in [[Bilming\|Bilming]] whose name suggests likely Terris heritage.
In 348 [[Catacendre\|PC]], Idkwyl made multiple [[Terris\|Terris]]-inspired designs. These designs were sold in a flagship store located in [[Bilming\|Bilming]]. Her designs were advertised in the *[[The Two Seasons\|The Two Seasons]]* broadsheet published on Marewill 19, 348 [[Catacendre\|PC]].
