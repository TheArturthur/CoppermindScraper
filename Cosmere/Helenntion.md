**Helenntion** was a member of the Council of [[Khlennium\|Khlennium]] on [[Scadrial\|Scadrial]]. He was one of the last survivors of the Council, although the timeline of Khlennium's dissolution after the [[Ascension of the Lord Ruler\|Ascension of the Lord Ruler]] is unclear.
[[Tindwyl\|Tindwyl]] found a useful piece of information in his biography while she and [[Sazed\|Sazed]] were researching the [[Terris Prophecies\|Terris Prophecies]]. The biography contained a direct quote from a prophecy that had previously been overlooked because the text used the Khlenni term *Rabzeen* to refer to the Hero of Ages.
