**Hinston Ladrian** is the son of [[Edwarn Ladrian\|Edwarn Ladrian]] and the heir to [[House Ladrian\|House Ladrian]] on [[Scadrial\|Scadrial]]. He is a cousin to [[Waxillium\|Waxillium]] and [[Telsin Ladrian\|Telsin Ladrian]].

## History
Prior to Hinston's birth, Edwarn had started training Wax and Telsin to be the heirs of House Ladrian, essentially raising them. Hinston was born to Edwarn and his wife as their first and only child in 317 [[Catacendre\|PC]], when Wax was eighteen. Once Hinston was born, Edwarn started preparing him to take over the house instead.
By around 340-341 [[Catacendre\|PC]], Edwarn felt that Hinston was ready to assume the title of High Lord of House Ladrian. Edwarn enacted plans to funnel House Ladrian's money to the [[Set\|Set]], intending to fake his own death and disappear. Once these plans were already in motion, Hinston succumbed to an unknown disease and died prematurely at the approximate age of 24. Edwarn could not abandon his plans, so he faked his death anyway and named Wax his successor instead, hoping that he could be manipulated.

## Legacy
A few weeks after Hinston's death, Edwarn, his wife, and Telsin went for a carriage ride in the [[Outer Estates\|Outer Estates]], supposedly to take their minds off of their grief. They took a carriage to a viewpoint because Hinston's mother was said to have been too weak for a hike. During the trip, their horses bolted and the carriage went over a cliffside. In fact, the accident had been contrived so that both Edwarn and Telsin could fake their own deaths. The fate of Hinston's mother is unknown, but it is likely that she died in the accident.
Edwarn planned for Hinston to take over House Ladrian even though he knew it would be destitute; Edwarn had also offended most of the other nobility in [[Elendel\|Elendel]]. Hinston had therefore been set up to fail, and was likely unaware of his father's behind-the-scenes dealings with the Set. Edwarn does not express much grief about Hinston's disease and untimely death, calling it "unfortunate".
