**Kasitor** is a coastal city in [[Iri\|Iri]] on [[Roshar\|Roshar]] and the home of a notable "[[Spren\|spren]]" known as [[Cusicesh\|Cusicesh]], the Protector.

## Geography
Kasitor is located in central Iri, in the northwest corner of the Rosharan continent. The city is situated on a bay, and it is not far from a small mountain range. The climate in Kasitor, like most of Iri, is very warm. It is the second most populous Iriali city—it has fewer residents than [[Rall Elorim\|Rall Elorim]], but more than [[Eila\|Eila]].

## Landmarks
  <<Cusicesh\|Cusicesh>> above the harbor
For more information, see [[Cusicesh\|Cusicesh]].
Kasitor's most well-known feature is a massive, unique magical entity known as Cusicesh, the Protector. Cusicesh resides in the city's bay; although he is called a spren in local parlance, he is actually a different type of [[Splinter\|Splinter]]. There is a viewing platform at the docks to accommodate the large crowds which congregate daily to observe Cusicesh emerging from the water, occuring at precisely seven forty-six in the morning. Four golden pedestals are also located in the bay where the spren's arms fall into the sea.

## Culture
The residents of Kasitor generally have long, golden hair that signifies pure Iriali blood. They wear minimal clothing due to the warm climate, and paint their exposed skin in various colors and patterns. Although they are not prudish, they are very particular about their chastity laws. They are generally a peaceful people.
The docks in Kasitor contain large ships, indicating that mercantilism is likely part of the local economy. Buildings in Kasitor are often made of bricks.
Cusicesh's presence has an influence on Kasitor's culture. Locals use Cusicesh's daily emergence to adjust their clocks. Though some worship Cusicesh as a god, most simply consider him to be a part of the city. [[Axies the Collector\|Axies the Collector]] notes that Cusicesh commonly has a "draining" effect on people.

## History
Around 1172, the [[Siah Aimian\|Siah Aimian]] known as Axies the Collector visited Kasitor as part of his efforts to research and observe spren. He intentionally got drunk while researching [[Alespren\|alespren]] and was beaten and robbed. He woke up nude in an alleyway next to a rather eccentric [[Beggar (Kasitor)\|beggar]]. Axies wrapped himself in a blanket while observing Cusicesh, but that too was stolen, and he was taken to the city's dungeons by some guards. He eventually escaped or was released.

### Long Trail
Shortly before [[Stormfall\|Stormfall]] in 1175, Cusicesh appeared at a different time than usual, faced his palms towards the city, and spoke directly to the Iriali in a sonorous voice. This appeared to be the first time he had exhibited any of these behaviors in the history of Iri, which by that point had existed for at least several millennia. He informed them that he would be the guide for the Fifth Journey in the [[Long Trail\|Long Trail]], a reference to the Iriali belief that their people would need to visit seven lands before becoming [[One\|One]].
Through unknown means, he opened a [[Perpendicularity\|perpendicularity]] that looked like a column of light; [[Dyel's mother\|Dyel's mother]] referred to it as "[[Honor's Perpendicularity\|Honor's Gateway]]". Cusicesh instructed the denizens of Kasitor that it was time to seek the Fifth Land, and the Iriali quickly prepared to leave Roshar forever. The specific results of Cusicesh's actions are unknown, but Kasitor presumably became deserted at this point. It is unclear if Iriali from other cities were able to enter the perpendicularity.
