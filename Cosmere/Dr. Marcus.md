**Dr. Marcus** is a psychiatrist in New York City who according to [[Lin Yang-Tanasin\|Lin Yang-Tanasin]], is the best in the city. [[Paul Tanasin\|Paul Tanasin]] is a client of Dr. Marcus as part of Paul's deal with his mother, to allow him to move out of home. Dr. Marcus has a pet cat called [[Leonard\|Leonard]] that likes everyone except for Paul, hinting at Paul's dark nature. Dr. Marcus is killed by [[Sir Ysla\|Sir Ysla]] during an emergency night time session with Paul, for allegedly serving the .

## Appearance
Dr. Marcus is a middle aged man with short, dark, combed over hair, and a square jaw. He typically wears a pair of large, half-rim glasses, and a black suit with a tie, though takes off the tie and jacket at night.
