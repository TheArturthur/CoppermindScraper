Lady **Tren-Pedri Delouse** is a [[Noble\|noble]] in [[Luthadel\|Luthadel]].
Her father wished to form an alliance with [[House Aime\|House Aime]] by marrying her off to one of the head of House Aime's sons. [[Vin\|Vin]] spread rumors through [[Kliss\|Kliss]] that Tren-Pedri's hesitance was because of Aime's ties to [[House Hasting\|House Hasting]], and with Hasting's fictitious withdrawal from [[Luthadel\|Luthadel]], Tren-Pedri wouldn't want to associate with them.
