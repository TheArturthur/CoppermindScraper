**Uli Da** is the original [[Vessel\|Vessel]] of the [[Shard\|Shard]] [[Ambition\|Ambition]]. She is referred to as female in the [[/wiki/Letters#First Oathbringer Letter]] sent to [[Hoid\|Hoid]]. She is a [[Sho Del\|Sho Del]], one of three races on [[Yolen\|Yolen]].

## History
 Nothing is known about Uli Da's origins and life prior to the [[Shattering\|Shattering]], other than that she came from Yolen. For unknown reasons, she and a group of others broke the [[Cosmere\|cosmere's]] creator deity, [[Adonalsium\|Adonalsium]], into sixteen pieces. Of those sixteen, she took up [[Ambition\|Ambition]], and then made her way to the [[Threnodite\|Threnodite]] system. Some of her fellow Shards immediately felt that she would be a "problem". These concerns seem to have originated primarily with her Shard's [[Intent\|Intent]].
For subsequent events, see [[/wiki/Ambition#History]].
Uli Da was mortally wounded by [[Odium\|Odium]] ([[Rayse\|Rayse]]) in a battle near Threnody. Ambition's power was then [[Splinter\|Splintered]] in an unknown location. The specifics of Uli Da's death and Ambition's destruction are not known, but these events had wide-ranging effects in the Threnodite system and across the cosmere.
