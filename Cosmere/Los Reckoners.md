***The Reckoners*** is a young adult series written by [[Brandon Sanderson\|Brandon Sanderson]]. The original series of books included *[[Steelheart\|Steelheart]], [[Firefight\|Firefight]], [[Calamity\|Calamity]]*, and a novelette, *[[Mitosis\|Mitosis]]*. An additional story about a different group of [[Reckoners\|Reckoners]], *[[Lux (book)\|Lux]]*, was released in July of 2021 as an audio original.

## Trivia
Brandon initially planned another series in the same universe, *[[The Apocalypse Guard\|The Apocalypse Guard]]*, about a group that works to save planets from mass extinctions. The first book, however, didn't work, and Brandon is currently working on revising with the help of his friend and author [[Dan Wells\|Dan Wells]]. He has not fully ruled out a connection to the Reckoners universe, but it is not a priority.
