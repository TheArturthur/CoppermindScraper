The **Ardra riots** are an event that destroyed most of the servers that could contain the contents of the vault at  vault. [[Tia\|Tia]] mentioned the riots to [[David Charleston\|David]] and [[Cody\|Cody]] in explaining why she was having trouble tracking down the contents of the vault.
