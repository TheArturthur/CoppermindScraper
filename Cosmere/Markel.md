The **markel** tree is a type of plant on [[Roshar\|Roshar]]. It can be found in [[Jah Keved\|Jah Keved]] and northwestern [[Alethkar\|Alethkar]].
It grows up to sixteen feet high and has large, clam-shaped leaves that can open and close. Some Vedens cultivate dwarf varieties of the tree. It has bark coated in stone and branches as "thick as a man's leg". [[Snarlbrush\|Snarlbrush]] grows in dense thickets around markel trees.
