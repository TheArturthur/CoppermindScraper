A **kattar**, pl. **kattari**, is a bladed weapon on [[Roshar\|Roshar]]. It is a thick triangular weapon somewhere between a dagger and a small sword in size. The kattar is held in one hand by a handle perpendicular to the blade, and can be thrusted with a pushing or punching motion. This makes them very useful in close confines, and they are popular in western Roshar. The blade can be ornate, and the weapon is small enough to be carried in a side sheath at the waist.
When [[Dalinar Kholin\|Dalinar Kholin]] visited [[Azimir\|Azimir]] in Jes 1174, the guards that accompanied [[Noura\|Noura]] were armed with kattari. Dalinar noted that he had always wanted one of the blades himself. [[Baxil\|Baxil]] also carried a kattar in Palah 1174 when he met with [[Axies the Collector\|Axies the Collector]].

## Trivia
The description of the kattar bears some similarity to a [[Thaylen\|Thaylen]] blade known as a [[Gtet\|gtet]].
A real-life triangular dagger from India known as the katar was originally known as a "kaţţāri" in the  language. Some examples of this real-life weapon look nearly identical to the one depicted in *[[Wind and Truth\|Wind and Truth]]*.
