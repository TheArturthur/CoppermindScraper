## Appearance and Personality
  Mirris and Vathi
Like most Aviar, Mirris is about three fists tall. She has subdued white and green plumage and is a breed of Aviar called a "streamer" which is native to some islands of the [[Pantheon\|Pantheon]], though not [[Patji (island)\|Patji]].
Mirris becomes aggressive towards strangers when injured, hissing at both [[Sixth of the Dusk\|Sixth of the Dusk]] and [[Kokerlii\|Kokerlii]] even when the former is applying care. Vathi sometimes comforts her by making clicking sounds.
When not in danger, Mirris is affectionate and nurturing toward both [[Rokke\|Rokke]] and Dusk.

## Attributes and Abilities
For more information, see [[Aviar\|Aviar]].
Mirris has a [[Nahel bond\|Nahel bond]] with Vathi and through it bestows on her the "talent" of concealing mental activity in her immediate vicinity; streamers being one of five breeds of Aviar to provide such an ability. It is likely that Mirris would also be able to conceal the use of some forms of [[Investiture\|Investiture]].

## History
  Vathi and Dusk take an injured Mirris into the trapper's safecamp
While working as a clerk for the [[Northern Interests Trading Company\|Northern Interests Trading Company]], Vathi brought Mirris on an expedition to Patji. The two joined a scout team searching for a site to place a fortress, but despite Mirris' presence, a [[Deepwalker\|deepwalker]] destroyed the rest of the team. Vathi subsequently decided that she must find a [[Trapper\|trapper]] to have any chance of survival and she and Mirris headed into the jungle.
Mirris was injured when she and Vathi fell after a "monster" attacked them and Vathi ended up caught in a [[Jellywire\|jellywire]] net outside of Dusk's [[Safecamp\|safecamp]]. Dusk later found them and was pleased to note that Vathi had applied some first aid to Mirris. However, Dusk believed that Mirris's wing was broken and brought the pair into his safecamp where he cleaned Mirris's wound, wrapped her wings to prevent flight, and placed her with his other Aviar to calm her down. Because of her injury, Mirris remained in the safecamp while Dusk and Vathi left to investigate the Company's activities.
Five years later, Mirris accompanied Vathi to a number of important meetings discussing the [[Ones Above\|Ones Above]] and the future of the [[Eelakin\|Eelakin]] people. She also traveled with Vathi back to Patji and through [[Patji's Eye\|Patji's Eye]], an experience the Aviar disliked, to see off Dusk before his journey. Mirris was also present when Vathi returned to Patji when Dusk returned.
