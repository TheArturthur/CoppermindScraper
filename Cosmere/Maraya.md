**Maraya** is a member of the [[Elendel\|Elendel]] Senate on [[Scadrial\|Scadrial]].
She was one of the senators [[Waxillium Ladrian\|Waxillium Ladrian]] attempted to persuade to vote against the [[Elendel Supremacy Bill\|Elendel Supremacy Bill]] in the year 348 [[Catacendre\|PC]], and he was unsure which way she would end up voting.
