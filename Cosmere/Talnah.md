**Talnah** is an engineer and [[Artifabrian\|artifabrian]] on [[Roshar\|Roshar]].

## Appearance and Personality
She is short, and her hair reaches past her ears. She wears a glove on her [[Safehand\|safehand]]. She is intelligent and naturally curious. She is friendly with her fellow scholars, including [[Nem\|Nem]] and [[Rushu\|Rushu]].

## Attributes and Abilities
Talnah is one of Queen [[Navani\|Navani]]'s most versatile and trusted scholars. She worked as a jeweler before turning to optical lenses, and is an expert on a variety of subjects including [[Polestone\|polestones]], lensmaking, weather readings, and astronomy.

## History
During the [[True Desolation\|True Desolation]], Talnah was a member of [[Navani\|Navani]]'s team of [[Artifabrian\|artifabrians]] and scholars in [[Urithiru\|Urithiru]]. In 1175, she and [[Nem\|Nem]] were tasked by [[Navani\|Navani]] with studying the [[Black sphere\|black sphere]] that [[Gavilar\|Gavilar]] had given to [[Szeth\|Szeth]]. At the time,  was not well-understood; as Talnah and Nem experimented with the sphere, they inadvertently caused an explosion that destroyed their lab, killing them both. Navani knew that she could not have reasonably expected a sphere to explode, but she still blamed herself for their deaths. She asked [[Rushu\|Rushu]] to perform a detailed forensic analysis on the lab.
