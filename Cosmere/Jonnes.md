Admiral **Jonnes** is a [[Malwish\|Malwish]] ambassador to [[Elendel\|Elendel]] on [[Scadrial\|Scadrial]] who is sent to Elendel sometime between 342 [[Catacendre\|PC]] and 346 [[Catacendre\|PC]].
She had a special place on the Elendel Senate, seated on the Senate floor with the visiting mayors of the [[/wiki/Elendel Basin#Outer Cities]]. Jonnes feared a possible war with the Basin. She was once seen visibly shaken after a speech made by Vice Governor [[Adawathwyn\|Adawathwyn]] about war with the Malwish, and she did not return to attend the rest of the session after its recess. During 348 [[Catacendre\|PC]], the leaders of the [[Malwish Consortium\|Malwish Consortium]] decided to send Admiral [[Daal\|Daal]] to Elendel to replace Jonnes as the Malwish ambassador. They believed that Jonnes had grown too familiar with the people of Elendel.
