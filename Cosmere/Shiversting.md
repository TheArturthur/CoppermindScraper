**Shiversting** is an . She had the ability to shatter metal objects, and possibly other things. The power leaves a slight black discoloration on the shattered pieces. With [[Parik\|Parik]]'s help, [[Lifeforce\|Lifeforce]] uses Shiversting's DNA to steal her power. Lifeforce later uses Shiversting's power to shatter [[Jax (Reckoners)\|Jax]]'s [[Shiloh\|sword]]. The power caused black veins to spread throughout the sword before it shattered.
