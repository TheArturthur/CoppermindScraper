**Deborah-James** is a child who lives on [[Beacon\|Beacon]] with her mother. When the [[Night Brigade\|Night Brigade]] arrives on [[Canticle\|Canticle]], Deborah-James is the first to notice their warship in the sky. She studies the stars in school for navigation purposes and is able to notice that the glowing warship is out of place.
