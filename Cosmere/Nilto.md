**Nilto** is a man in [[Kezare\|Kezare]] also known as the "Lord Beggar".
His face appears disfigured and is covered in wrappings. He is an acquaintance of the  [[Loaten\|Loaten]]. [[Ais\|Ais]] believes that Nilto is the crime lord known as [[Sharezan\|Sharezan]].
