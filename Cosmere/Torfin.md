**Torfin** is a member of [[Bridge Four\|Bridge Four]] on [[Roshar\|Roshar]].

## Attributes and Abilities
After Kaladin swears the [[Immortal Words\|Third Ideal]] of the [[Order of Windrunners\|Order of Windrunners]], Torfin becomes one of his [[Squire\|squires]]. He is able to breathe in [[Stormlight\|Stormlight]] whenever Kaladin is nearby, giving him healing powers and the ability to fly using the [[Surge\|Surge]] of Gravitation. Unfortunately, he is scared of heights.
By Chach 1175, Kaladin no longer has any squires, and "almost all" of the original Bridge Four members have bonded a [[Windspren\|windspren]]. This implies that Torfin is a full [[Knight Radiant\|Knight Radiant]].

## History
Torfin has been a member of Bridge Four since its origins as a wartime bridge running crew under [[Torol Sadeas\|Torol Sadeas]]. How he came to be a bridgeman is unknown.
After the crew was sent on chasm duty in the [[Shattered Plains\|Shattered Plains]] to loot [[Parshendi\|Parshendi]] corpses, they were quiet and nervous, but Kaladin engaged Torfin, [[Drehy\|Drehy]], and other members of the crew in conversation to break the tension. Torfin was later seen training with the rest of Bridge Four in the warcamps. When [[Moash\|Moash]] abruptly stopped carrying a bridge, Torfin yelled at him, not realizing that Moash stopped due to his shock at seeing Kaladin's rapid recovery after his judgement by the [[Highstorm\|highstorm]].
Torfin was in [[Narak\|Narak]] when the [[Alethi\|Alethi]] began occupying the area around Jes 1174.
