**Ruli** is one of [[Taravangian\|Taravangian's]] granddaughters on [[Roshar\|Roshar]]. She has a gap-toothed smile.
In a letter sent to Taravangian while he is in [[Urithiru\|Urithiru]], [[Karavangia\|Karavangia]] enclosed a sketch of herself, Ruli, and [[Gvori\|Gvori]]. Ruli had included a small drawing of some flowers. Ruli is not the grandchild who was trapped following a rockfall.
