House **Yomen** is a major socio-political entity on [[Scadrial\|Scadrial]] after the [[Final Ascension\|Final Ascension]]. They are counted as a powerful house, with the majority following the religion of [[Sliverism\|Sliverism]]. The Yomens are known to follow [[Sliverism\|Sliverism]]. This likely stems from Aradan Yomen's status as an [[Obligator\|Obligator]] during the [[Final Empire\|Final Empire]], and his worship of [[The Lord Ruler\|The Lord Ruler]] and his [[Steel Inquisitors\|Steel Inquisitors]]. Notable members include [[Aradan Yomen\|Aradan Yomen]], [[Joshin Yomen\|Joshin Yomen]], and [[Mi'chelle Yomen\|Mi'chelle Yomen]].

## Property
The house owns a large [[Yomen mansion\|mansion]] in [[Elendel\|Elendel]] city, which was the location of a notable Yomen [[Yomen-Ostlin wedding dinner\|wedding]]. They also have several smaller property investments in [[Elendel\|Elendel]]. Joshin Yomen in particular bought run-down buildings and renovated them, causing crime to drop substantially in the area.

## History
### After the Catacendre
House Yomen was established by Aradan Yomen, or his descendants, shortly after the Catacendre. The House quickly grew in power, becoming a great noble House. It also got a seat on the Elendel Senate as soon as the latter was established.

#### Yomen-Ostlin wedding
During the year 341 [[Catacendre\|PC]] Lord Joshin Yomen married Lady Mi'chelle Ostlin. The [[Yomen-Ostlin wedding dinner\|wedding]], a large event attended by most of Elendel’s upper crust, was hosted at Yomen manor. During the first course the wedding was robbed by [[Miles Dagouter\|Miles Dagouter]] and the [[Vanishers\|Vanishers]], who kidnapped [[Steris Harms\|Steris Harms]] and temporarily Marasi, killed Lord [[Peterus\|Peterus]], and stole plenty of jewelry from everyone, including the members of House Yomen themselves, with the most noteworthy possession stolen being the bridal necklace.

## Politics
As a powerful Noble House, the Yomens have a seat on the Elendel Senate. House Yomen, through senator Mi'chelle Yomen, was the first house to vote against the [[Elendel Supremacy Bill\|Elendel Supremacy Bill]]. Lady Yomen is also known to be a good friend to [[House Ladrian\|House Ladrian]]. This is likely a direct effect of Wax and his allies' heroic actions at the [[Yomen-Ostlin wedding dinner\|Yomen-Ostlin wedding]], as well as his involvement with the investigation and capture of the Vanishers.
