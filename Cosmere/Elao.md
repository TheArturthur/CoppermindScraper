**Elao** is an [[Arelene\|Arelene]] servant in King [[Iadon\|Iadon's]] palace on [[Sel\|Sel]]. She is an old woman. Her name is based on [[/wiki/Aon#Ela]], meaning focus or center.
She brought [[Raoden\|Raoden]] fruit on the morning when the [[Shaod\|Shaod]] took him, and was the first to see Raoden in this form. Upon seeing Raoden, she swore to [[Domi\|Domi]], trembled, and grabbed the [[Korathi\|Korathi]] pendant she wore. She then hastily ran away.
