**Futinoro** is a city in [[Nagadan\|Nagadan]] on [[Komashi\|Komashi]] that is completely destroyed by a force of several dozen stable [[Nightmare\|nightmares]] thirty years prior to the [[Battle of Kilahito\|battle of Kilahito]].
To the people of Nagadan, the attack is believed to have been the consequence of [[Painter\|painters]] not showing proper vigilance at their jobs. It stands as a warning sign to the dangers of not halting nightmares before they become fully stable. According to [[Liyun\|Liyun]], who is present during the city's destruction, the attack is directly orchestrated by the [[Father machine\|father machine]] to curb the efforts of some [[Hijo\|spirits]] that managed to contact the residents of Futinoro. The machine has the entire city destroyed in order to prevent the truth from being discovered.
