**Ala** is a [[Seon\|seon]] on [[Roshar\|Roshar]].

## Appearance and Personality
Like all seons, Ala appears as a glowing ball of light, with her namesake [[/wiki/Aon#Ala]] at the center.
Upon being released from [[Mraize\|Mraize's]] communication cube, she initially acts like an abused child, as if traumatized by the [[Ghostbloods\|Ghostbloods]]. She is reluctant to come out of the cube or speak to anyone and shows a dim light and flickering Aon. However, that is a ploy to hide the fact that she is working with the Ghostbloods. Once the deception is over, Ala restores her light and Aon to full brightness and displays disgust and derision about having had to be around [[Shallan\|Shallan]] and [[Adolin\|Adolin]].

## History
[[Mraize\|Mraize]] sent Ala, hidden in a metal cube, to [[Shallan\|Shallan]] for use as a cross-realm communication device during the [[/wiki/Lasting Integrity#Urithiru Emissary Delegation .281175.29]]. In order to prevent Shallan from learning the true nature of the communication device, Mraize instructed her not to open the box. During the trip, Shallan spoke to Mraize through Ala, updating him on the status of the mission. Meanwhile, [[Pattern\|Pattern]] was secretly using Ala to speak to [[Hoid\|Hoid]] via another seon, probably [[Olo\|Olo]]; Shallan noticed that someone was moving the cube, becoming paranoid about a spy on the mission until Pattern revealed everything to her. Ala was also spying on Shallan's other conversations during the trip, which allowed [[Iyatil\|Iyatil]] to later imitate [[Formless\|Formless]] to surreptitiously follow Shallan in the [[Spiritual Realm\|Spiritual Realm]].
After Shallan officially broke off ties with the Ghostbloods, she and [[Kalak\|Kalak]] were able to free Ala from the cube without harming her. Ala acted as if she had been abused by the Ghostbloods to gain the sympathy of Shallan and the others. Shallan used Ala one last time to speak to Hoid, then left her behind with Kalak at Lasting Integrity. Kalak held on to Ala and the cube until [[Felt\|Felt]] arrived to capture him, at which point Ala revealed her true allegiance. She was in favor of killing Kalak immediately so she could leave [[Roshar\|Roshar]], but Felt convinced her to hold off so they could extract more information from Kalak, which they reported back to Mraize through another seon the next day.
Some months after [[Stormfall\|Stormfall]], Ala and Felt were camped out near [[Haka'alaku\|Haka'alaku]]. Shallan tracked them down and demanded that [[Thaidakar\|Thaidakar]], speaking through Ala, send Ala to work with her as recompense for what the Ghostbloods had done to her; Thaidakar and Ala agreed, as long as Ala could send reports back to Thaidakar. Shallan's first request of Ala was to contact Olo, with whom Ala had already been chatting, to ask him to go to [[Azimir\|Azimir]] and make contact with Adolin.
